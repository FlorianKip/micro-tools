# Legal References for GEG §72 Calculator

## Primary Sources

### §72 GEG — Betriebsverbot für Heizkessel
**URL:** https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/geg/__72.html

Key provisions:
- Abs. 1: Oil and gas boilers operated for >30 years must be replaced
- Abs. 2: Applies to Konstanttemperaturkessel only
- Abs. 3: Deadlines based on municipality size (>100,000 inhabitants)

### §73 GEG — Ausnahmen vom Betriebsverbot
**URL:** https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/geg/__73.html

Key provisions:
- Abs. 1 Nr. 1: Low-temperature and condensing boilers are exempt
- Abs. 2: Owner-occupied properties where owner lived there since before Feb 1, 2002

### §97 GEG — Aufgaben des bevollmächtigten Bezirksschornsteinfegers
**URL:** https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/geg/__97.html

Key provisions:
- Chimney sweeps enforce compliance during mandatory inspections
